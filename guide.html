The First Step

When planning your visit you must first check Saudi Arabia's offical website to see if you meet the entry requirements.

    The 2 basic requirements are:

  • Have a valid passport for 6 months
  • Be from one of the elidgeable countries

    • If perferoming Hajj you must also have:

  • Vaccination certificates
  • Negative PCR test and register at https://arrival.muqeem.sa/ 72 hours before leaving your counrty.
